Brand Story

A name that began with soil, seasons, and patience.

Samchodam does not try to rush the time it takes for red ginseng and marsh orchid to grow. The brand is shaped by subtraction: fewer distractions, clearer ingredients, and a slower, deeper finish.

Wellness does not need to feel complicated. If the origin, the process, and the taste all make sense, the choice can stay simple.
